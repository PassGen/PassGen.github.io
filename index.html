    Advantages of using our app

    1. It is completely offline so you don’t have to worry about your data falling in the wrong hands
    2. Generated passwords can be regenerated by following the same steps as you did for the original password (but only on the same device)
    3. Generated passwords can be stored as bitmap images of corresponding QR codes after being encrypted by an in-house encryption scheme
    4. You can create unlimited number of passwords and store the QR codes. Just remember the PIN that you enter to encrypt your passwords and your passwords will be hiding in plain sight in your gallery.
    5. All passwords are procedurally generated, and are unique for each device. The same app on two different devices won’t generate the same password with the same set of inputs.
